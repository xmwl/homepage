Mobile Wallet for iOS and Android
Product, Design, Prototyping

One of the bigger projects at Raise was updating the design for the wallet redemption screens in both the iOS and Android apps. Serving as the way for customers to use their gift cards in-store, the screen is unique in that is serves two types of customers, Raise users and the cashiers that are redeeming gift cards.

Working with the UX team to get user feedback and testing, I used the feedback from customers and cashiers to create a simpler, easier to read, and better branded wallet redemption screen. I went multiple iterations on designs and prototypes to arrive at the current design.

Design, Prototyping

The ability to sell unwanted gift cards has been an important feature of the Raise iOS app since the app‚Äôs launch. It had never been updated even though many parts of the app had changed around it.

My Receipts for Android
Product, Design

Rate the App for iOS
Product, Design

The goal with this project was to build a feature within the iOS app that would allow a Raise user to give Raise a rating on the App Store or provide feedback for improvement.
